Slice Scanner

Volcoholic

play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=iandroid.volcoholic
Volcoholic is an alcoholic beverage comparison app designed to help you get the maximum amount of alcohol for your money. Just enter the details of the beverages on offer and the app will sort them based on their strength, volume and price. I used Phonegap for this initially but then went with Ionic as it seemed to have some nicer features.
github.com/ianluddy/volcoholic
